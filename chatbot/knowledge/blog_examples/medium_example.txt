Title: The Hidden Costs of Creative Burnout You Can’t Ignore

Burnout isn’t just exhaustion—it’s a silent drain on innovation, productivity, and mental health.

## What’s Behind It?
- Pressure to produce constantly
- Lack of rest or meaningful breaks
- Unchecked expectations in creator culture

## My Realization
After burnout hit, I realized that my relentless pace was applauding creativity only at the cost of well-being.

## How to Prevent It
- Build in mental health recovery time  
- Work asynchronously where possible  
- Support team wellness and downtime

Burnout isn’t a badge of honor—it’s a sign to reset.
